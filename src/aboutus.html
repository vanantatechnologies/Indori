About us

Our Values

Yummy Indori Food Pvt Ltd is a modern food brand deeply rooted in the vibrant food culture of Indore, India. Specializing in authentic Indori flavors, we bring the true essence of street food and traditional snacks to every bite. With high-quality ingredients, a commitment to preserving authentic tastes, and warm hospitality, we create inviting spaces that connect people, celebrate Indore’s rich culinary heritage, and offer an unforgettable food experience—one dish at a time.

Your perfect blend of flavor and comfort

Our Mission

At Chai Nimki, our mission is to craft moments of comfort and connection through the perfect blend of tradition and innovation. We strive to deliver an exceptional tea and snack experience rooted in authenticity, freshness, and care. By upholding the values of Indian hospitality and using only the finest ingredients, we aim to create spaces that bring people together - where every sip of chai and every bite of nimki becomes a memory worth sharing.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish Chai Nimki as a trusted and beloved brand that redefines India's tea culture on a national and global stage. We aspire to blend the charm of local street flavors with world-class quality and design, creating a brand that resonates with both tradition and modernity. Through sustainable growth, consistent innovation, and an unwavering focus on authenticity, Chai Nimki envisions becoming a symbol of India's warmth, taste, and timeless togetherness - one cup at a time.

Why Chai nimki

Chai Nimkii reimagines India's tea culture with a modern twist. We blend tradition with quality, offering authentic chai paired with delicious snacks, creating a warm, inviting space for community connection.

Great Taste, Always

Every cup and snack is crafted for consistent, rich flavor using quality ingredients, ensuring a satisfying taste experience every time you visit Chai Nimkii.

Affordable Prices

Friendly Café Vibe

Trusted by Thousands
